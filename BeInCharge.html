According to Business Insider, at the lowest levels, more than half of the employees in organizations are female. As you move to each successively higher level in the organization, the number of women steadily shrinks. At the CEO level, worldwide, there are only 3% to 4% who are women. We believe that women can and must take charge within higher levels in order to change perspectives and the way in which women are perceived within our world.
"Women are leaders everywhere you look, from a CEO to a housewife that holds together a home. Our country was built by women who stand alone."
-Denise Clark, member of The CLark Sisters
